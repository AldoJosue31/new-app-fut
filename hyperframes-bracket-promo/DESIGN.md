# Bracket Promo Design

## Visual Identity
Bracket is a football league management app with a dark, technical UI, cyan primary actions, white data text, compact cards, and tournament metrics. The promo should feel like a professional sports control room: focused, energetic, and clean.

## Colors
- App background: `#131F24`
- Panel surface: `#10191D`
- Primary cyan: `#1CB0F6`
- Bright cyan: `#38D4FF`
- Success green: `#53B257`
- Warning gold: `#F5A400`
- Text: `#F6FAFC`
- Muted text: `#A9BBC4`
- Border: `#263740`

## Typography
- Headlines: system `sans-serif`, 800-900 weight, large and compact.
- Data and labels: system `monospace`, 500-800 weight, tabular numbers.
- Body/UI copy: system `sans-serif`, 500-700 weight.

## Motion
- Smooth camera pushes on real interface captures.
- Bracket-line wipes and compact scoreboard flashes between sections.
- Cursor movement should be controlled and minimal.
- Text enters with varied GSAP eases; no repeated entrance pattern.

## What Not To Do
- Do not invent app screens or features.
- Do not show credentials or login typing.
- Do not use chaotic glitching, generic stock scenes, or heavy full-screen gradients.
- Do not alter the captured UI beyond zooms, masks, and readable overlays.
